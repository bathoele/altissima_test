Authentic theatre. Artist-run. Student-led.

Founded by college artists, Altissima Studios is a theatre startup created to build opportunities where performers can create honestly. Our debut: The Last Five Years (Jason Robert Brown) — mid‑April 2026.

Who we are

We’re students, artists, and producers who wanted a more direct path from rehearsal room to stage. Altissima Studios focuses on intimate, musician-forward productions and meaningful collaboration between performers and creative teams.

Our first production

We present The Last Five Years by Jason Robert Brown — an intimate two-person musical exploring love across time. Performances scheduled for mid‑April 2026. Sign up for updates and auditions.

Get involved

We’re recruiting performers, collaborators, and volunteers. If you’re a musician, actor, or stagehand who wants to create meaningful work, reach out — we’d love to meet you.
